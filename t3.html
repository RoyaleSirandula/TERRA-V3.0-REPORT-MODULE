Solutions · Target Customers

Target
Customers

01
Large-Scale Conservation NGOs
Organisations like Save the Elephants managing vast protected territories spanning hundreds of thousands of hectares, operating with limited ranger coverage and disconnected field data systems.
Core Need
Real-time, AI-verified intelligence to eliminate fragmented data silos and enable non-technical teams to make instant, evidence-backed field decisions.
02
Government Wildlife Authorities
National parks agencies and wildlife services responsible for enforcing conservation law across protected areas — often burdened by analog VHF radio systems and spreadsheet-era reporting workflows.
Core Need
Drastically reduced response times to poaching and HWC incidents, replacing hours of latency with seconds-level situational awareness.
03
Local & Private Conservancies
Community conservancies managing human-wildlife boundaries, where livestock losses and crop destruction erode community support for conservation.
Why Terra Matters Here
Terra converts communities from passive bystanders into active intelligence contributors — farmers submit real-time sightings, receive early-warning alerts, and gain a direct stake in conservation outcomes.
